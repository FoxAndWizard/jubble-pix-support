Account and Data Deletion Request

Welcome to the data management page for Jubble Pix.

We respect your privacy. If you wish to permanently delete your account and all associated data from our servers (including your linked Google/Facebook profiles, game progress, scores, and device identifiers), please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

To initiate the deletion process, please send us an email directly to our support team. We require this manual step to verify your identity and ensure your game data is not deleted accidentally.

Email your request to: [TWÓJ_EMAIL_SUPPORTU]

Subject Line: Account Deletion Request

What to Include in Your Email

To help us locate and delete your data as quickly as possible, please include at least one of the following in your email:

  • The Email Address you used to sign in via Google or Facebook.
  • Your exact in-game Username.
  • Your Support/User ID (if you are playing as a guest via Device ID, you can usually find this in the game's settings menu).

What Happens Next?

Once we receive your request:

  1. We will locate your account in our database.
  2. We will permanently erase your profile, game progress, and any linked social IDs or device identifiers within 7 business days.
  3. We will send you a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Please note: This action is irreversible. Once your data is deleted, your game progress cannot be recovered.
